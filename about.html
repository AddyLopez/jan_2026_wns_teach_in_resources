About Workers for a New Southwest (WNS)

"WNS exists to build a fighting socialist movement in New Mexico. We take New Mexico's real conditions--scarce water, rising rents, land loss, and a war and extraction economy--and treat them as the ground for new working class institutions. We aim to link labor fights, tenant work, and community defense into one front. We reject the idea that workers need either duopoly party, nonprofits, or wealthy patrons to act. We push people to move as a class with their own institutions, strategy, and discipline." (Excerpt from pamphlet, "About WNS")

Get in touch with an organizer to learn more about who we are, our three points of unity, and how to join us!
